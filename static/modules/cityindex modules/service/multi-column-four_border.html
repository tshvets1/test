Key Reason to Trade Indices

Indices are popular because they allow traders to trade a group of shares that represent the health of a nation’s economy, allowing traders to speculate on a nation’s economy with one trade, rather than picking individual stocks.

Diversify Your Risk

Trade a whole basket of industries in one trade, rather than an individual stock.

Extensive Media Coverage

Indices receive considerable coverage and there is easy to access information about these markets.

High Liquidity

High liquidity in indices means tight spreads and leverage.

24 Hour Trading

Many indices are available to trade 24 hours, so you are not just limited to stock market opening hours.
