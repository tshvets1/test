How to Sign Up

City Index offer spread betting and CFD trading on indices. Whilst similar, typically UK investors choose spread betting because profits made are free from Capital Gains Tax (CGT).

1. Complete an application

Once you submit your application we process your details and you could be up and running in minutes.

2. Fund your account

Upon approval of your account you can login and fund it via debit card, credit card or wire transfer.

3. Start trading

Find the markets you want to trade, or use our analysis tools for trade ideas and inspiration.
